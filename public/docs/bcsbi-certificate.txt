## Six Principles of BCSBI Code

### Principle I: Integrity and Fairness
Banks shall deal with customers fairly, honestly and transparently.

**MNS Bank Commitment:**
- Complete transparency in all dealings
- Fair treatment of all customers
- No hidden charges or fees
- Honest communication of product features

### Principle II: Transparency
Banks shall provide clear and transparent information to customers.

**MNS Bank Commitment:**
- Clear product terms and conditions
- Transparent fee structure
- Complete interest rate information
- Detailed risk disclosure

### Principle III: Right to Privacy
Banks shall respect customer privacy and confidentiality.

**MNS Bank Commitment:**
- Data protection policies
- Confidential customer information
- Secure data storage
- Limited data sharing

### Principle IV: Right to Fair Treatment
Banks shall treat all customers fairly and without discrimination.

**MNS Bank Commitment:**
- Non-discriminatory practices
- Equal opportunity services
- Fair grievance redressal
- Respect for customer dignity

### Principle V: Right to Suitability
Banks shall offer products suitable to customer needs.

**MNS Bank Commitment:**
- Needs-based product recommendations
- Risk profiling
- Suitability assessments
- Appropriate product advice

### Principle VI: Right to Grievance Redressal
Banks shall have effective grievance redressal mechanisms.

**MNS Bank Commitment:**
- Dedicated grievance cell
- Timely complaint resolution
- Fair investigation process
- Customer-friendly procedures
