## Types of Complaints Covered

### 1. Deficiency in Banking Service
- Delay in collection of cheques, drafts, bills
- Non-acceptance of cheques without sufficient reason
- Non-credit of proceeds to customers' accounts
- Failure to honour commitments

### 2. Credit Card Issues
- Billing disputes
- Unauthorized charges
- Delay in issuing credit cards
- Failure to provide statements

### 3. Loan Related Issues
- Delay in sanction/disbursement of loans
- Non-adherence to fair practices code
- Excessive interest charges
- Forced insurance premiums

### 4. Deposit Account Issues
- Delay in opening/closing accounts
- Unauthorized deductions
- Interest calculation errors
- Statement of account issues

### 5. Remittance Issues
- Delay in remittance facilities
- Non-adherence to exchange rates
- Failure to provide remittance advice
- Charges not disclosed

### 6. Mobile Banking Issues
- Failed transactions
- Unauthorized access
- Service disruptions
- Technical issues

## Complaint Process

### Step 1: File Complaint with Bank
- Contact MNS Bank customer service
- File written complaint
- Wait for bank response (30 days)
- Keep complaint reference number

### Step 2: Approach Banking Ombudsman
- If unsatisfied with bank response
- File complaint within 1 year
- Use prescribed form
- Attach supporting documents

### Step 3: Ombudsman Review
- Ombudsman reviews complaint
- May call for additional information
- Provides recommendation
- Bank implements recommendation

---

## Supporting Documents Required

### Mandatory Documents
1. Complaint form (duly filled)
2. Bank's reply (if any)
3. Proof of transaction (if applicable)
4. Identity proof of complainant

### Additional Documents (if applicable)
- Account statements
- Loan documents
- Credit card statements
- Correspondence with bank
- Receipts and vouchers
- Any other relevant documents

## Important Instructions

### Before Filing Complaint
1. Approach the bank first with your complaint
2. Wait for bank's response (maximum 30 days)
3. Keep all documents and correspondence
4. File complaint within 1 year of bank's response

### Filing Procedure
1. Fill complaint form completely
2. Attach all supporting documents
3. Submit to appropriate ombudsman office
4. Keep copy of complaint and documents

### Timeline
- Acknowledgement: Within 7 days
- Resolution: Within 30-60 days
- Extension: Maximum 90 days in complex cases
