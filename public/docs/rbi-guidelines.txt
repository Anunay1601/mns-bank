## 1. Know Your Customer (KYC) Guidelines

### Objective
To prevent banks from being used, intentionally or unintentionally, by criminal elements for money laundering activities.

### Key Requirements
- Customer identification procedure
- Monitoring of transactions
- Risk management
- Customer acceptance policy

### Implementation
MNS Bank implements robust KYC procedures including:
- Document verification
- Biometric authentication
- Regular customer due diligence
- Transaction monitoring

## 2. Anti-Money Laundering (AML) Standards

### Framework
Comprehensive AML framework to detect and prevent money laundering activities.

### Components
- Customer due diligence
- Transaction monitoring
- Suspicious transaction reporting
- Record keeping

### Compliance
- Regular AML audits
- Staff training programs
- Regulatory reporting
- Risk assessment

## 3. Digital Banking Guidelines

## 4. Interest Rate Guidelines

### Fair Practices
- Transparent interest rates
- Clear communication of charges
- No hidden fees
- Competitive pricing

### Disclosure Requirements
- Annual percentage rate (APR)
- Processing fees
- Prepayment charges
- Late payment penalties

## 5. Customer Service Standards

### Service Quality
- 24/7 customer support
- Multi-channel service
- Prompt complaint resolution
- Service level agreements

### Grievance Redressal
- Dedicated complaint cells
- Escalation procedures
- Timely response
- Fair resolution

## 6. Data Protection Guidelines

### Privacy Protection
- Data encryption
- Secure storage
- Limited access
- Regular audits

### Customer Rights
- Right to information
- Right to correction
- Right to deletion
- Right to portability
